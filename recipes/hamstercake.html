Hamster's 1st Birthday Cake

Description

This is a hamster-friendly cake that you can make for your hamster's first birthday!

Ingredients

  • fruits cut into hamster-friendly size
  • a pinch of flour
  • sufficient water
  • the soul of a small spider

Steps

  1. Stir flour and water to make dough.
  2. Bake in oven for 5 mins at 180 degrees celsius.
  3. Garnish with fruits and soul of a small spider.
